Blond Roux

A basic sauce used in making several dishes, from cheese sauce to Sweedish meatballs

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup of butter
  • ½ cup of flour

Steps

  1. Place a saucepan over medium heat. Then add butter and stir until melted.
  2. Reduce heat to low. Whisk in flour, 1 tablespoon at a time, and cook, whisking constantly.
  3. Once the roux becomes fragrant yet does not take on much color. The dish is complete.
